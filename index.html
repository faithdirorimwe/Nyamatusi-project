Our story

Nyamatusi Camp is our flagship in terms of its Eco-conscious style. Our Mana Pools safari lodge is set in one of the wildest and most remote part of Africa, on the bank of the Zambezi, one of the world's oldest river. Embracing comfort, this property embodies sustainable designs. Fully solar-powered and built from all-natural material encompasses its wildness location in the most elegent way. Here, your Mana Pools safari plays out against a sweeping riverine landscape, where epic encounters of the gaints that roam this land awaits.
